Our Love Story

It all began in 2003

during our first-year high school periodical exam. While sitting outside the SBO office, a simple glance through a window led to an unexpected moment, we met eyes, and that was where everything started. He approached me and introduced himself, a fourth-year high school student at the time. Back then, I didn’t like him at all in fact, I could say he was the person I liked the least.

A year passed

I became a second-year student, and he had already graduated. Despite that, he came to my home and courted me with sincerity and patience. On April 8, 2005, just before the school year ended, I finally said yes. We shared countless motorcycle road trips and created many memories together. Our journey was not always easy we faced challenges and difficult times but through it all, we held on to each other.

And now, after 21 years of love

Growth, and unwavering commitment, here we are inviting you to witness the beginning of our forever. I saw someone beautiful, and out of curiosity, I asked a classmate for her name and year level. From that moment, it was love at first sight. I decided to pursue her, even though she was strong, reserved, and not easily won over.

A WEDDING TO REMEMBER: A DAY OF JOY, FAMILY, AND PROMISES KEPT

Courting her was not easy, but I remained patient and sincere. On April 8, 2005, while we were walking from the gym, she finally said yes. After graduating from high school, I made it a point to pick her up from school every day and walk her home, staying by her side until she finished her studies. When she moved to General Santos, we faced a brief long-distance relationship. But I found a way to be closer to her—applied for a job there, determined to keep our love strong. Eventually, we built a life under one roof, and now, at last, we are preparing to say “I do.” With joyful hearts, we invite you to witness the beginning of our forever. 💍
